Page 1 💜 Friends first 🤝 Everyone joins Safe Soulmates to make friends. 💛 That is the most important thing. 😊 Not everyone wants a romantic relationship. 👍 That is OK. 💚 Friendship is just as special. 🤝 Safe Soulmates is not a dating agency. 📋 We cannot promise everyone a relationship. 💛 But we will always be honest and kind to you. 🐌 Take it slow. 📵 Nobody likes lots of messages all at once. 💚 Friendship first. Slow and steady. Page 2 🛑 Saying no 🗣️ "No thank you" is a full sentence. ❓ You do not need to explain why. 💚 Your "no" will always be respected. ✋ If someone says stop, you must stop. 😄 It does not matter if you think it is funny. 💔 It might really hurt the other person. 🚫 If you do not stop, you may have to leave the group. 🙋 If someone keeps messaging you after you said no: 🔇 You can block them. That is OK. 📞 Tell Vicky, Christine, or Amanda too. Page 3 ✋ Consent 💬 Consent means someone says a clear yes. 🤗 This is for hugs, touching, photos, and meeting up. 🤐 Quiet is not a yes. 😟 If someone looks unsure, that is not a yes. ❓ Always check. Wait for a clear yes. 🔄 A yes before does not mean yes now. 🛑 People can change their mind any time. ✅ If they say stop, you stop straight away. 😣 Never push someone to say yes. 😞 Asking again and again is still pressure. 🚓 Not listening to "no" is very serious. 🚫 It can mean leaving the group, or the police. 💛 If something felt wrong, tell us. We will listen. Page 4 🎉 Going to events 😊 Come as you are. 🚪 You do not have to talk to everyone. ⏰ You do not have to stay the whole time. 📍 Always tell someone where you are going. 💷 Pay for your own food and drink. 💧 You can just bring water and your own food. 0️⃣ You don't need to spend any money. 🧸 Bring what helps you feel comfortable. ✋ Fidgets, comfort toys, support animals — all OK. 🚶 Take a break whenever you need to. 🌈 We meet lots of different people. 💜 Everyone is a bit different. That's OK. 🐢 Please be patient with others, and with yourself. 🙋 Support workers and carers are always welcome. Page 5 📱 WhatsApp groups 🕘 Only send messages 9am to 9pm. 😴 Late messages can stop people sleeping. 🌅 There are special groups for early risers and night owls. 👍 The usual times don't apply in those groups. ☀️ No "good morning" or "good night" messages. 📵 Too many messages can feel like a lot. 👀 You do not have to read every message. 🔇 You can mute the group any time. 💬 Only message someone privately if they say yes first. 📸 Only share kind and safe photos and videos. Page 6 🛡️ Keeping safe 🔒 Do not share your address, bank details, or passwords. ⚠️ Some people pretend to be your friend to trick you. 📞 If you think it's a scam, call Action Fraud: 0300 123 2040. 📷 Think before sending photos of yourself. 🔋 Keep your phone charged. 🚨 In an emergency, call 999. It is free. 🥤 Never leave your drink alone at events. Page 7 💡 What is triggering? 😰 Triggering means something causes a big, sudden feeling. 😨 It might feel like fear, panic, or sadness. 💛 This is real. It is never "being dramatic." 🗯️ A joke that feels funny to you might hurt someone else. 😐 Some people find it hard to show feelings. 🤔 This is called alexithymia. 💚 Someone may look calm but feel upset inside. ✅ If someone says stop: 🛑 Stop straight away. 🙏 Say sorry if you can. 😊 Move on kindly. Page 8 💬 We are here for you 🙋 You never have to deal with anything alone. 💛 Talk to any member of staff. We will listen.
